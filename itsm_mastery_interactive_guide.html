5. The Future: Agentic AI & ESM

The shift from assistive AI (search/summaries) to agentic AI (autonomous goal-driven agents) is transforming ITSM into Enterprise Service Management.

🤖

Autonomous Squads

Multi-agent squads utilizing MCP (Model Context Protocol) to hand off tasks across silos without human intervention.

🛡️

Guardrail Systems

Policy controls and 'human-in-the-loop' validation checkpoints to prevent AI from triggering cascading infrastructure failures.

🌐

Unified ESM

Applying ITSM logic to HR, Facilities, and Finance via a single 'Super Portal' for a consistent employee experience.
