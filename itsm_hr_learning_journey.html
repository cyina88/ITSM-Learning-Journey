Current Functional Gaps
⚠️ Always validate these before scoping an engagement. These are known platform limitations and gaps as of June 2026.
⚠️ FlagArchitect · SC

HAM — Hardware Asset Management

NOT YET GA. Do not scope Hardware Asset Management in any current engagement. Flag as roadmap item only.

Status: Roadmap
⚠️ FlagArchitect

Single-Org HR + IT

Roadmap only. Today HR Service and IT Service require separate employee orgs. Single-org design is not available. Separate employee org required in all current engagements.

Status: Roadmap
⚠️ FlagArchitect · SC

Case OWD — Must Be Private

Mandatory design gate. Case OWD must be set to Private for ER and HR cases. Non-negotiable security requirement — affects the entire sharing model design.

Status: Mandatory
⚠️ FlagSC · Architect

MS Teams — Known Gaps

Teams lacks Incident Broadcast and full Swarm parity vs. Slack. Teams-only customers cannot get the full ITSM Slack experience. Raise early in discovery.

Status: Known Gap
⚠️ FlagSC · Architect

CMDB — Separate SKU

CMDB is not included in the base IT Service Desk SKU. Configuration Item relationship topology and service impact analysis require a separate CMDB license. Always clarify in scoping.

Status: Separate SKU
⚠️ FlagSC

People Manager Workflow

Active product gap. No native People Manager visibility/approval workflow in HR Service today. Must be custom-built. Flag in discovery when customer requires manager-in-the-loop HR flows.

Status: Product Gap
